Features

  • Hotswap PCB
  • Programmable with QMK and Via
  • Rotary encoder
  • Optional sound dampening
  • USB-C connectivity
  • High recyclability

Technical specifications

Supported layouts

Box contents

  • Case top and bottom
  • Plate
  • PCB with pre-soldered buttons and rotary encoder
  • Fasteners
  • Screwdriver
  • Feet for flat and 2° typing angle
  • Rotary encoder knob
  • Sound dampening
