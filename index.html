KB-40-A

A low-frills, 40% keyboard kit with a unique stainless steel sheet metal construction. And light-up buttons.

Features

  • Hotswap PCB
  • Programmable with QMK and Via
  • Optional sound dampening
  • No wireless hassles to waste your time on
  • Limited options so your brain doesn't melt
  • USB-C connectivity

Box contents
